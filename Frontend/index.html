Welcome to KeyCheeky

KeyCheeky is a simple yet secure Password Management System designed to store and protect users’ passwords efficiently.

The platform allows users to save, view, edit, and delete their account credentials safely using an encrypted database handled through a C backend.

Get Started

About KeyCheeky

Our goal is to help users manage all their passwords in one place without compromising on privacy or usability. With an intuitive interface and reliable encryption logic, KeyCheeky ensures that your passwords stay safe — and only you have access to them.

Future upgrades aim to include multi-user support, two-factor authentication, and mobile/desktop app versions for greater flexibility.
